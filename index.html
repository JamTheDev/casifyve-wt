Best Silog Meals in Town

Dishes

Kim's Hungry Potters Tapsilogan is a popular restaurant located in a beautiful setting, where you can enjoy a wide variety of delicious dishes served at your table. Not only will you be able to savor the flavors of the dishes you choose, but you will also be able to take in the stunning view and ambiance of the place. The restaurant has a cozy and inviting atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for a casual dinner with friends or a romantic date.

Some of the dishes that people always come back for include tapsilog, chicksilog, porksilog, sisigsilog, and bangsilog, which are all made with the freshest ingredients and cooked to perfection. Each meal typically includes 2 servings of garlic rice, a sunny-side up egg, and a main dish of your choice.

Be sure to stop by and try out the delicious food and great atmosphere at Kim's Hungry Potters Tapsilogan which is are affordable and sure to fill your tummy, leaving you satisfied and happy.
