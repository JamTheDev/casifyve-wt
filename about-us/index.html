About Us

Hungry Potter Kim's Tapsilogan is one of the most visited best silog meals in town, located at 37-E A.Luna St. West Rembo Makati City, Makati, Philippines, with approximately 2,000 followers on their active Facebook page.

CasiFYVE is a team of five 1st year college students that collaborated and helped power this website. These young computer science students are: J. Fernandez, C. Magtajas, MA. Siazon, JE. Villarosa, and J. Yoldi.

As their final project in Web Development Tools at the University of Makati (UMak), we would like to thank our professor, school, and this business for the opportunity and the knowledge we learned as a team along the journey. From contacting, researching, collaborating, coding and more, it was a worthwhile journey that we hope to help this business thrive more in the digital age.
