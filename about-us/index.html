About Us

Hungry Potter Kim's Tapsilogan is a popular destination for delicious silog meals, located at 37-E A. Luna St. in West Rembo, Makati City. The restaurant has approximately 2,000 followers on its active Facebook page.

CasiFYVE is a team of five 1st year college students who have collaborated to help power the restaurant's website as their final project in Web Development Tools at the University of Makati (UMak). The team members are J. Fernandez, C. Magtajas, MA. Siazon, JE. Villarosa, and J. Yoldi.

We are grateful to our professor Mr.Pahayahay and the University of Makati for the opportunity to work with Hungry Potter Kim's Tapsilogan and contribute to its success in the digital age. It has been a rewarding journey filled with learning and growth. We look forward to continuing our partnership in the future and are thankful for the valuable experience we gained through this project.
